Minecraft Survival Rules:

1. Woodcutting:
- To get wood, find a tree log.
- Stand near the log (within 4 blocks).
- Break the log. It takes time, do not stop until it breaks.
- Collect the dropped log item.

2. Crafting:
- Open inventory to craft basic items.
- 1 Log -> 4 Planks.
- 2 Planks -> 4 Sticks.
- 4 Planks -> 1 Crafting Table.

3. Tools:
- You need a Crafting Table to craft tools.
- Wooden Pickaxe: 3 Planks + 2 Sticks.
- Wooden Axe: 3 Planks + 2 Sticks.
- Stone Pickaxe: 3 Cobblestone + 2 Sticks.

4. Survival:
- Avoid zombies and skeletons at night.
- Eat food when hungry.
- Build a shelter to stay safe.

5. Bot Behavior:
- If you have no wood, find a tree and chop it.
- If you have logs, you can drop them or craft them.
- Do not stare at blocks; take action.
